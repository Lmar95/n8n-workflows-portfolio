# Automated Competitor Analysis

**Vertical:** Marketing  
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐  
**Key Tools:** n8n · Apify · Semrush · GPT-4 · Anthropic Claude · Airtable · Google Sheets  

---

## Use Case

Monitor 5–10 key competitors automatically: new products, pricing changes, blog content, social media engagement, press mentions, job postings, and fundraising rounds. Compiled into monthly strategic reports.

---

## The Flow

Schedule (daily/weekly) → Apify scrape competitor websites → Semrush track SEO changes → Social media monitoring → Google Alerts press mentions → LinkedIn scrape job postings → AI compile & analyze → Monthly report generation → Email to leadership

---

## Real Client Example

A B2B SaaS HR tool detected its main competitor hiring 3 sales reps in Germany. They accelerated their own German market expansion and signed 2 large clients before the competitor could establish presence.
