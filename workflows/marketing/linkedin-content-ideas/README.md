# LinkedIn Content Ideas Generator

**Vertical:** Marketing  
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐  
**Key Tools:** Phantombuster · n8n · Taplio · Shield Analytics · Notion · LinkedIn API  

---

## Use Case

Analyze top-performing posts in your niche daily (by hashtags and key influencers). Identify winning topics, formats (carousels, case studies, hot takes), and underexplored angles. Generate 20–30 post ideas per week with hooks, structure, and CTAs tailored to your positioning.

---

## The Flow

Daily: Phantombuster scrape top posts by hashtags → Taplio pull engagement analytics → AI analyze: what topics, formats, angles perform best → Generate 20-30 ideas with hooks + structure + CTA → Push to Notion content calendar → Weekly digest to creator

---

## Real Client Example

A growth marketing consultant receives 25 post ideas every Monday based on the week's trending topics. He selects 5, personalizes in 1h. In 4 months: +8,000 followers, 3 inbound clients/month from LinkedIn.
